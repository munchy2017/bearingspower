Company Overview

Bearings & Power Transmission (Pty) Ltd, a company that is held by ArcelorMittal (50%), Kalahari Resources (40%) and IDC (10%) has been involved in the exploration for Manganese in the Kalahari Basin. The three farms on which the company holds new order mining rights are believed to overly some 960 million tons of manganese ore. The recent exploration programme has identified 102 million tons of Mineral Resources.
